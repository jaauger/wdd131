Sunbeam Home Solar Guide

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Sunbeam Home Solar Guide

The name “Sunbeam Home Solar Guide” represents a clear, friendly resource for homeowners who want simple, trustworthy information about residential solar power. It suggests clarity, sunlight, and guidance — the purpose of the entire website.

Optional domain availability example: sunbeamsolarhomeguide.com

Site Purpose

This website provides homeowners with a beginner-friendly introduction to residential solar power. It explains how solar panels work, the components of a solar system, what solar installations typically cost, and the financial incentives available. Visitors will be able to understand the basics before speaking with a contractor or installer.

Scenarios

  • “How do solar panels actually work? I want a simple explanation.”
  • “How much does a solar system cost, and is it worth it for my home?”
  • “What equipment do I need for a home solar system?”
  • “Are there tax credits or incentives I can qualify for?”

Color Schema

The website will use a clean, solar-themed color palette.

Solar Gold – #F7B733
Used for accents, buttons, and highlights.

Sky Blue – #4A90E2
Used for headings and section titles.

Deep Slate – #2F2F2F
Used for footer background and body text.

Light Gray – #F1F3F4
Used for card backgrounds and section contrast.

Typography

The site uses Noto Sans from Google Fonts. It is clean, modern, and highly legible.

  • Headings (h1–h3): Noto Sans, 700 weight
  • Body text: Noto Sans, 400 weight
  • Navigation & UI labels: Noto Sans, 500 weight
  • Captions & small notes: Noto Sans, 300 weight
